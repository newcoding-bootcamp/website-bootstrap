Full-Stack Software Development Course

Offered for Credit through Ventura County Office of Education

In this 18-week course offered for credit, students will learn modern web development technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6, git, GitHub, node, React, and PostgreSQL database.

The course culminates in building a personal project full-stack web application.

Ages: Middle School through Junior College   Cost: Free

Class: Wed 6:30-8:30p, August 29, 2018 - January 23, 2019

Homework: 3 hours per week. Computer and internet access outside of class is required

Location: Computer Lab C3-104, Rancho Campana High School

Instructors & Mentors

Join Our Team

NewCoding is powered by a core team of volunteer instructors and mentors. We're always looking for tech professionals willing to make at least one 3-hour commitment each quarter on a Wednesnday night from 6-9p.

Instructors

Individual instructors teach our curriculum workshops. Workshops typically consist of code-along instruction followed by independent student exercises.

Mentors

Each workshop, several mentors float around the classroom, assisting students one-on-one. This includes answering questions, debugging, and teaching the tooling.
